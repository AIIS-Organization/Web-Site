The Artificial Intelligence Implementation Show is a two-day conference which aims to provide insight into the latest developments of AI models and their applications in the industry. In collaboration with universities and companies from the tech space, this event will present the mechanisms behind these models and their everyday usage. Join this event to be part of a new community that encourages AI education!
